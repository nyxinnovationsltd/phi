# PhiVerse Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

## Overview

PhiVerse ("the App") is an astrology app that provides personalized insights based on Vedic astrology. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Birth Information
- Date of birth
- Time of birth
- Place of birth

This information is required to generate your astrological chart and is essential for the App's core functionality.

### Device Information
- Device identifier (for iCloud sync)
- App usage data (locally stored)

### No Third-Party Tracking
We do **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

## How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Birth information | Generate personalized astrological charts and readings |
| Device ID | Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud |

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage:** Your data is stored securely on your device using Apple's Keychain
- **iCloud Sync:** If enabled, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **No External Servers:** We do not store your personal data on external servers

## AI Chat Feature

The App includes an AI chat feature powered by third-party AI services (OpenRouter/Google AI). When you use this feature:
- Your astrological chart data may be sent to generate personalized responses
- We do not send your full birth details to AI services
- Conversations are not stored on external servers

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete your data** at any time through the App settings
- **Disable iCloud sync** to keep data only on your device
- **Request data export** by contacting us

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
